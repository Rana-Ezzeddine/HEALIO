FOR patients who need to manage their health, caregivers (non-medical individuals, a family member or paid helper who regularly looks after a patient), and doctors or healthcare providers who provide medical treatment 
WHO require a secure and organized way to track health data, communicate, and coordinate care 
THE HEALIO is a healthcare management and coordination system 
THAT enables patients, caregivers, and doctors to interact through role-based access, health tracking, and secure communication
UNLIKE basic health-tracking applications or fragmented medical portals that only allow limited viewing of data 
OUR PRODUCT provides a unified platform where patients control their medical information, caregivers assist in daily care (not medical actions), and doctors manage multiple patients efficiently.
